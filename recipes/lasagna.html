Lasgna Recipe

Lasagna recipe found under a 200 Y.O. house

An easily customizable recipe so that you can include the ingredients you like the most!

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage and Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasgna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
